Computation Reference

Bloom's Taxonomy Weighting:

T1 = CUI × 0.6              (Understanding - 60% weight)
T2 = CDI × 0.2              (Debugging - 20% weight)
T3 = CCI × 0.2              (Completion - 20% weight)

CL1 = T1 + T2 + T3          (Technical Composite Index)
CL1_Div3 = CL1 / 3          (Average across indices)
CL1_Div3_Scaled = CL1_Div3 × 0.5  (Scaled technical proficiency)
