About EEG Data

EEG (Electroencephalography) is a non-invasive technique that measures electrical brain activity. Our platform integrates EEG analysis with cognitive performance metrics to provide comprehensive insights into:

  • Neural correlates of cognitive load and performance
  • Brain activity patterns during learning and problem-solving
  • Temporal dynamics of cognitive processing
  • Individual differences in neural efficiency
  • Real-time cognitive state assessment
